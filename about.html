About Typeless

Typeless is a free service that lets you bring all your contacts (Gmail, Outlook, iPhone, Android etc.) from anywhere into one place and share them easily and securely with your friends and colleagues, accessible from any device. It also enables you move specific contacts into other software apps like Salesforce, Quickbooks or Mailchimp - wherever you need them.

Typeless was founded in 2013 by Satish Jayakumar, Michael Stephanblome, Alok Srivastava and Sudhanshu Saxena with the vision of making it easier to have your contacts always accessible, up-to-date and shareable with others.
