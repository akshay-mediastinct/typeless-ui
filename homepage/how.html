About us

Typeless is a platform for professionals to collaborate on contacts. Typeless imports, cleans & unifies all your contacts from multiple sources including Gmail, Facebook, Outlook, Linkedin, Salesforce & many others.

Use Typeless to connect & collaborate on contacts with people that you trust. By giving & getting contacts with each other, you & your connections are able to drastically increase the size of your contact book while also reducing the amount of time it takes to get in touch with someone whose details you do not have.
