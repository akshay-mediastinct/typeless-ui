Chrome extension for GMail

With Typeless you have all your contacts right in your Gmail inbox.

Search by name, company or job title and see the profile of everyone who emails you.

Share contacts with your friends and colleagues and request contact details.

Chrome extension for Office 365

If you are using Mail on Office 365 on the web you can use the Chrome extension for Office 365.

It helps you to have all your contacts (and access to your friends and colleagues' contacts) — even when you are on the go using a different computer.


Outlook 2010/2013 app

The Typeless Outlook App helps you to have all private and business contacts right in your inbox.

You can share your contacts with friends and colleagues inside and outside your company and create a virtual contact network to get to people faster.

iOS app

Our iPhone app will enable you to have all your mobile phone contacts on iOS together with all your other business and private contacts.

It will enable you to sync seamlessly between the iPhone native contact app and the Typeless iPhone app.


Android app

Our Android app will help you to have all your local mobile phone contacts together with all your other business and private contacts.

It will also enable seamless syncing between the native contacts app of Android and the Typeless Android app.
