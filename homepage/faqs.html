What is Typeless?

Typeless is a service that lets you manage and collaborate on contacts.

Typeless unifies all your contacts, no matter where they come from: Google contacts from Gmail or Android devices, Facebook, Linkedin, Salesforce, Microsoft Outlook and many more. And yes, Typeless dedupes and stitches these contacts into one enriched contact.

Typeless lets you share your contacts with your friends and colleagues. You can share all your contacts or only some of them. You can share only names and job titles of your contacts, so the people you share them with will have to request them on Typeless. It's secure and easy to amplify your contact network to thousands of new connections, and you can stop sharing at any time.

Are my contacts safe?

Typeless data runs on 1024 bit encryption making it enterprise grade secure.

All the data between you and Typeless’ servers are SSL encrypted with 1024-bit keys, making it one of the strongest encryptions possible. We are also ICO registered.

Do I always need to go to www.typeless.co to access my contacts?

No, you can have Typeless in your inbox and in your mobile device. That way you can access all your contacts (even shared ones) whenever and wherever you need them.

We have already released an extension for Chrome users to use within GMail and a Microsoft Outlook 2010/2013 app. When typing an e-mail Typeless will prompt you from all your contacts, not just the ones you have used in that email account. When reading an e-mail, Typeless will show you relevant contact details from the contacts in that conversation.

iOS and Android apps are coming very soon.

What contact sources does Typeless support?

Currently Typeless supports Google Contacts (Gmail and Android devices), Facebook, Linkedin, Office 365 and Salesforce. Typeless will support iOS contacts when the app will be available.

We are constantly adding new contact sources, so please get in touch if you didn’t find the one you use.

Will Typeless save contact details back to the contact source?

No, Typeless will just get contacts from contact sources and merge them only inside Typeless.

The original data in the contact sources will remain untouched, so nothing will be synced back.

What ways can I add contacts to Typeless?

The easiest way is by connecting contact sources to Typeless, so that contacts will be kept updated.

You can also add new contacts manually or import them from a .CSV file and we will still merge them. Unmerged manually added contacts won’t be kept updated.
